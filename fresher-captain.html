Fresher Captain and Fresher Guide

The Fresher Captain is the life of the party. The Captain is there to introduce new students to student life and the culture on campus. There are many unwritten traditions that exist that the Fresher Captain introduces. You can spot the Fresher Captain via the peculiar clothes they typically wear, one of which is typically a construction helmet.

You can approach and interact with the Fresher Captain anytime, and you’re even encouraged to, especially at parties. If you have any issues or want to ask questions, the Fresher Captain can point the way or answer questions about things such as traditions.

Contacting the Fresher Captain is easy and you shouldn’t feel nervous to do so. Just look for or ask for Samut Jokela or contact him via email.
