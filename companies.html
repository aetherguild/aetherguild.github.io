For companies

Any questions or propositions should be sent to our Corporate Relations Manager’s email: corporate@aetherguild.fi

We are open for company visits and excursions, to plan one out please contact our Excursion Master and the Project Manager via email: events@aetherguild.fi

For companies that are giving away food and drink products as sponsorships we request you contact us via email: corporate@aetherguild.fi

We are always open and eager for cooperation; even the smallest contribution matters to us, and we make sure to return the favor.

We aim to have 1 event per month. We typically have a mix of non-alcoholic and alcoholic events, but people are encouraged to drink responsibly even at alcoholic events. We don’t tolerate peer pressure when it comes to drinking and we wish to make a safe and comfortable environment for everyone.

To contact the Project Manager/event organizer email events@aetherguild.fi

Sponsorships related to Slounge are flexible and can be done via email: corporate@aetherguild.fi
