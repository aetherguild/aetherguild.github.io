LUT Career Services

Are you striving for your dream job? If so, active engagement is crucial. With the help of the versatile lut career services, you can build your career path from the beginning of your studies. Gain access to the wide range of services through JobTeaser using your LUT email address and password. Take the first step by registering now at lut.jobteaser.com. Discover essential information and insights from the JobTeaser Info Bank. Whether you're seeking details about employment opportunities, professions, or graduate salaries, this is the ideal starting point. Additionally, you'll find valuable resources on the Finnish working life and receive tips to enhance your employability.

Enhance your skills on the Find Your Career self-study platform. Explore various areas such as crafting a compelling CV, mastering job interviews, searching for employment, and refining your professional skills. Alongside these, receive personalized feedback on your job-hunting documents. This service is available to you 24/7 via Moodle throughout your academic journey.

Utilize the JobTeaser job portal to explore a diverse range of opportunities. Discover permanent positions, part-time jobs, internships, and thesis work specifically targeted at our students. Moreover, access a wide selection of trusted international job openings beyond Finland's borders. Expand your professional network by participating in our recruitment events. These events provide an excellent platform to establish connections that can aid in your future job search. Attend the career recruitment events on campus which include the Lahti Careerlink, DuuniExpo, and DuuniDay at the Lappeenranta campus, along with some more smaller events.

Acquire valuable knowledge through training sessions, webinars, and group coaching. Collaborate with fellow students to develop essential job-hunting and career planning skills. Schedule an appointment for personalized career counseling. Whether you are still exploring your career path or facing challenges in your job search, the dedicated team at the LUT career services is here to assist you every step of the way.
