About us

History

Aether ry is a student guild made by the students for the students. It was formed early in 2022 by a group of students that wanted to bring an energetic culture to the Lahti campus. It serves all degree programs in Lahti. This has led to a student culture that tries to be inclusive toward people of different backgrounds. The goal of Aether is to make a comfortable environment for students to study in. Aether tries to ensure a bright future for everyone studying at LUT.

Aether also hosts events, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. We promote a healthy lifestyle by making sure people don’t feel any pressure to drink. We make sure to adopt both new and old traditions, such as the famous sitz that Finnish guilds have.

Interesting facts about Aether:

  • The color of the overalls is officially golden poppy yellow (#FCC200), and black (#000000)
  • The official mascot of Aether is a stuffed toy chicken that is called “Mafia Chicken”
  • Aether was the first guild to establish at LUT Lahti, which was on 3.3.2022
  • Aether focuses mainly on creating a good environment to study and prosper in
  • Aether is focused on making inclusive and healthy events where drinking isn’t mandatory and where peer pressure isn’t tolerated, and events where physical activity takes place to promote wellness

Our mission

The purpose of AETHER ry is to bring together students and support them in both their professional and social interests. The guild plays an active role in the development of teaching content and helps new students adjust to university and Teekkari life. Additionally, the guild is committed to promoting equal treatment of all students and works to make the transition to university for incoming freshmen as smooth as possible.

Supporting wellbeing

Promoting and maintaining the physical, mental, and emotional health of our members.

Professional development

Helping our members build their careers and prepare for their future careers.

Academic advocacy

Advocating for the academic interests of our members and ensuring that their voices are heard in the decision-making process.

Community

Building a sense of community among members, fostering relationships, and encouraging social interaction.

Safety and equality

Monitoring the equal treatment of students and working to ensure a safe environment for all.

Student culture

Preserving and promoting student culture in Finland, by providing opportunities for students to learn about and engage in traditional Finnish student activities and traditions.
