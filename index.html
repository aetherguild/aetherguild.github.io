Our mission

The purpose of AETHER ry is to bring together students and support them in both their professional and social interests. The guild plays an active role in the development of teaching content and helps new students adjust to university and Teekkari life. Additionally, the guild is committed to promoting equal treatment of all students and works to make the transition to university for incoming freshmen as smooth as possible.

Supporting wellbeing

Promoting and maintaining the physical, mental, and emotional health of our members.

Professional development

Helping our members build their careers and prepare for their future careers.

Academic advocacy

Advocating for the academic interests of our members and ensuring that their voices are heard in the decision-making process.

Community

Building a sense of community among members, fostering relationships, and encouraging social interaction.

Safety and equality

Monitoring the equal treatment of students and working to ensure a safe environment for all.

Student culture

Preserving and promoting student culture in Finland, by providing opportunities for students to learn about and engage in traditional Finnish student activities and traditions.
