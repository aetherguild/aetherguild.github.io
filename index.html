About the guild

AETHER ry is a student guild based in the Lahti campus of LUT University. The guild works to create a supportive community for students and organizes events and activities that promote student engagement and create a sense of belonging.

  • Learn more

The board and officers

The board of AETHER ry consists of 10 members, who are responsible for the guild's operation. In addition, there are various officers assisting the board. Each has their own specific duties and responsibilities that contribute to the smooth functioning of the organization.

  • Learn more

For members

By joining AETHER ry, students could ensure that their voices are heard and their opinions are valued. The guild also offers a variety of events and fun activities for its members, providing opportunities for socializing and building relationships with others who share similar interests.

  • Learn more

Our mission

The purpose of AETHER ry is to bring together students and support them in both their professional and social interests. The guild plays an active role in the development of teaching content and helps new students adjust to university and Teekkari life. Additionally, the guild is committed to promoting equal treatment of all students and works to make the transition to university for incoming freshmen as smooth as possible.

Supporting wellbeing
