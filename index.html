About us

AETHER ry is a student guild based in the Lahti campus of LUT University. The guild works to create a supportive community for students and organizes events and activities that promote student engagement and create a sense of belonging.

  • Learn more

For members

As a member of AETHER ry, students can participate in the guild's activities and events, and they can also benefit from the guild's services. The guild also provides a platform for students to voice their opinions and concerns.

  • Learn more

For freshers

By joining AETHER ry, students could ensure that their voices are heard and their opinions are valued. The guild also offers a variety of events and fun activities for its members, providing opportunities for socializing and building relationships with others who share similar interests.

  • Learn more

For companies

By cooperating with AETHER ry, companies can reach out to students and promote their services and products. Students will learn valuable skills and gain experience, and companies will have the opportunity to recruit new employees.
